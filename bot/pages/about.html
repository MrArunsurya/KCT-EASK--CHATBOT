About Kumaraguru College of Technology

Kumaraguru College of Technology (KCT), located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India, is an autonomous engineering college affiliated with Anna University.

Established in 1984, KCT has grown into one of the leading engineering colleges in India, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering and technology.

The college is known for its strong emphasis on research, innovation, and entrepreneurship. It has state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus community that nurtures academic excellence and holistic development.

KCT is committed to producing socially responsible engineers and technocrats who can address the challenges of the future and contribute positively to society.

Key Features:

  • NAAC Accredited with 'A' Grade
  • Autonomous Institution
  • Industry Collaboration and Internship Programs
  • Research and Innovation Centers
  • Entrepreneurship Development Cell
